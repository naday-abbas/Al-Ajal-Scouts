Muharram Ul Haram


Muharram-ul-Haram is a sacred month, and we are honored to serve during Majalis, Juloos, and religious gatherings. Our duties include managing security, guiding attendees, and ensuring a smooth flow of events. Special focus is given to Ashura (9th & 10th Muharram-ul-Haram), where we assist in maintaining order and supporting mourners.

Jashan


Jashan celebrations mark the joyous occasions of Wiladat, commemorating the birth anniversaries of revered personalities. In the months of Rajab and Shaban, we actively participate in organizing and managing events such as Jashan-e-Mairaj, Jashan-e-Imam Ali (AS), Shab-e-Baraat, and Jashan-e-Wiladat-e-Imam Mahdi (AS).

Medical Camp


During religious gatherings and processions, many devoted participants engage in Matam, often sustaining wounds that require immediate medical attention. At Al Ajal Scouts, we are committed to ensuring their well-being by setting up Medical Camps where we provide first aid, bandages, wound cleaning, and necessary medical support.

About Us

Al Ajal Scouts is a vibrant community of young individuals dedicated to personal growth, leadership, and service..

Our Mission

"Empowering youth to grow, lead, and serve their community with dedication and enthusiasm. Our mission is to build future leaders who are equipped with essential life skills."

Our History

"Founded to foster leadership and teamwork, Al Ajal Scouts has been serving the community for years. Through various activities, we've grown into a hub of learning and growth."

Our Activities

"Organizing community service events, outdoor adventures, and leadership development programs for young scouts. We provide hands-on experiences that help scouts learn valuable skills in real-world settings."

Our Values

"Committed to integrity, responsibility, teamwork, and respect for nature and people. These core values guide every action, ensuring we foster a positive impact in the lives of all involved."

Our Vision

At Al Ajal Scouts, our mission is to serve the Momineen with unwavering dedication, expecting nothing in return but the love and blessings of Ahlulbayt (A.S). We strive to be a symbol of security and service at religious events, ensuring a safe and organized environment for all. Our commitment is driven by faith, unity, and the values taught by the Holy Household. Through our efforts, we aim to create a legacy of selflessness, devotion, and brotherhood in the path of Ahlulbayt (A.S).

S.Zamir Hussain Shah
